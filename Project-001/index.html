About Me

I am Warlie Potenciano, a family man, a husband to a beautiful loving wife and a father to a cute naughty little boy. I am currently residing in Sta. Rosa Laguna .

My hobbies are playing Mobile Legends, traveling, biking, eating and watching series or movies. Before the pandemic, I also loved to travel and was in love with biking. I also love to eat local foods whenever I travel. I’ve been to various places in the Philippines, South Korea, Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand and Japan and I want to add more on the list, soon, with a little bit of change, because I would want to travel with my wife and son given the chance and time to travel again. Since I have been mostly at home ever since the pandemic, I enjoyed watching the series of Lucifer, Big Bang Theory and How I met your Mother.

I am a Mechanical Engineer by profession. I am now 31 years old and I believe it is not too late to jump into something new. I’ve been planning lately to shift my career into something that interests me, which is somehow related to what I am doing in my work, that needs my critical thinking and strategic planning. I am an organized person and I always think and plan ahead of time before taking an action because I believe that when you fail to plan, you plan to fail. I’ve been loyal to my company where I am currently employed, as I celebrate this year my 10th year anniversary as their employee. This has been my bread and butter and never changed company because the working environment is healthy which allowed me to grow professionally as to who I am today.
